# Daily Ritual

**System:** ICT-SMC PO3-AMD — Silver Bullet V1
**Rule:** If morning preparation is skipped → do not trade.

---

## Morning Routine (~30 minutes, pre-session)

### 1. Mindset Check (2 min)
- [ ] Am I calm, focused, and patient?
- [ ] Am I trading process, not P&L?
- [ ] Am I NOT trading to recover losses?

**IF NO to any → no trade today.**

### 2. High-Impact News Check (3 min)
- [ ] Economic calendar reviewed (Benzinga Pro or Investing.com)
- [ ] No surprise high-impact events during London or New York session
- [ ] Note any events within ±30 min of planned entry window

**IF high-impact news during 10:00–11:00 ET → no trade in that window.**

### 3. Session Selection (1 min)
- [ ] London (overnight session)
- [ ] New York (primary — 10:00–11:00 ET Silver Bullet window)

**No other sessions.**

### 4. HTF Preparation (10–15 min)
- [ ] Mark PDH / PDL (Previous Day High / Low)
- [ ] Mark PWH / PWL (Previous Week High / Low)
- [ ] Identify HTF Points of Interest (POIs)
- [ ] Define directional bias OR range condition

**Tools:** Premarket Levels, OTE-OR-HTF-PO3

### 5. PO3 Narrative (5 min)
- [ ] Where is accumulation likely?
- [ ] Where would manipulation occur (liquidity to be swept)?
- [ ] Where is distribution expected?

**IF no clear PO3 narrative → Gate 3 will fail. Prepare to stand down.**

### 6. Execution Readiness (5 min)
- [ ] 6-gate A+ checklist visible (`Current_System_Map.md`)
- [ ] Risk rules reviewed (RR ≥ 1:2, per-trade risk 0.5–1.5% equity)
- [ ] "No trade" is an acceptable outcome today

### 7. Focus of the Day (1 min)

Current phase:
- [ ] Migration / platform setup (Weeks 1–2)
- [ ] Backtest / paper trading (Weeks 3–6)
- [ ] Apex evaluation / funded execution (Weeks 7–13)

---

## Evening Routine (~20–30 min, post-session)

### 1. Session Close (2 min)
- [ ] Stop trading when rules say stop
- [ ] No late or emotional trades after the window closes

### 2. Journal Entry (10–15 min)
- [ ] Log all trades AND no-trades in TradeZella
- [ ] Apply correct tags: A+ / Rule-break / Emotion
- [ ] Attach HTF + LTF screenshots to each entry

### 3. Checklist Audit (2 min)
- [ ] Did I follow all 6 gates?
- [ ] Any gate bypassed? (Yes / No — be honest)

### 4. Emotional Review (2 min)
- [ ] State: Calm / Frustrated / Confident / Fatigued
- [ ] Was emotion present before or after the outcome?

### 5. One Improvement Only (2 min)
- [ ] One specific behavior to improve tomorrow
- [ ] No system overhauls or rule changes in the moment

### 6. Closure (1 min)
- [ ] Today is complete
- [ ] Tomorrow starts fresh

**Rule: A day without journaling does not count as a trading day.**

---

## Daily Identity Statement

*Read once in the morning, once in the evening:*

**"I execute my plan with discipline. I protect capital first. Consistency is my edge."**

---

## Non-Negotiable Rules

1. **A+ setups only.** If gates 1+2+3+4(required)+5+6 fail → no trade.
2. **Capital protection first.** Daily loss limits are final. No revenge trading.
3. **RR ≥ 1:2.** Risk per trade = 0.5–1.5% equity. Never more.
4. **Journal everything.** Trades. No-trades. Emotions. Mistakes.
5. **"No setup is better than a forced setup."**
